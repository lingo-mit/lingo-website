## Reading Group

We run a reading group in the lab. Each round has a
theme chosen by group vote, and for a few weeks we read 
papers, textbook chapters, and other useful background around that theme.

Some sessions have invited speakers; others are internal discussions around a
paper we read together.

We list sessions here as an archive of what we have read and a snapshot of
what we are currently thinking about.
